OUR PROFILE

Afya ya watoto wetu

The Kenya Paediatric Association is an association of Kenyan paediatricians. We also have some researchers, paediatricians in training and other health workers on board. The association was registered in 1968 and seeks to make Kenya a country where children achieve physical, mental and social well being in the absence of disease or infirmity.

Since registration we have gone, and continue to go on about this in several ways, among which are:

  • Disseminating knowledge concerning the causes, treatment and prevention of childhood diseases by any means deemed expedient.
  • Playing significant roles in counseling support in the fight against societal ills such as drug abuse, HIV/AIDS, Rape etc.
  • Encouraging research into causes, management and prevention of childhood diseases in Kenya.
  • Collaborating in research advocacy education training and sharing experiences for the implementation of best strategies.
