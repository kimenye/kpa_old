Benefits to Individual Members

  1. Chance to network and work with other health workers, to share experiences and create exchanges leading to mutual benefits for members of KPA.
  2. Access to objective, diverse information on medical issues through participation in conferences (usually held once a year) and other interactive gathering organized for members from time to time by Kenya Paediatric Association management.
  3. Representation before policy makers such as the government to lobby for the interest of the members.
  4. Young professionals will get mentorship opportunity by mingling with the experienced health workers who are members of the association.
  5. An opportunity to electronically receive medical updates as and when KPA management receives them.
  6. A chance to receive the KPA newsletter.
  7. An invitation to continuous medical education programmes at no cost and earn Continuous Professional Development (CPD) points as KPA is accredited by the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentist Board (KMPDB).
  8. An opportunity to participate in annual child health advocacy events.
  9. Access to the resource centre for reference materials.
  10. A link with International Paediatric Association, as KPA is a member of International Paediatric Association (IPA) and Union of National African Paediatric Societies and Associations (UNAPSA).
